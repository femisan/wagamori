All Wagamori content must comply with Japan's 景品表示法 and ステマ規制 (stealth-marketing rules).

Rules:
- NO fabricated reviews, fake testimonials, or invented numbers/stats.
- NO exaggerated/unprovable claims (e.g. "完全再現", "絶対", "業界No.1").
- AI-preview images must be presented AS previews/illustrations — never passed off as a real customer's photo.
- The before→after card assets (test-assets/ig/card-*.png) depict finished products; captions must not imply they are unsolicited real customer reviews. Frame as brand examples / "イメージ" where relevant.
- Quoted "customer voice" style lines (e.g. 「わたしの絵だ！」って大よろこび) must be written as illustrative scenarios, not attributed as real testimonials, unless the user supplies a genuine, consented review.

**Why:** Hard constraint from the brand owner; fines + platform/reputational risk under JP law.
**How to apply:** Vet every caption before proposing. Prefer "色合いまでできるだけ忠実に" over "完全再現". Keep all outward actions (post/comment/follow/DM) gated on explicit per-action user approval. Never auto-publish.
